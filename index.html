ABOUT

Hi, This is our Home Website! We are students at HTW Berlin University developing a smart bot to assist with disease diagnosis.

Introducing AuaChat, your AI-powered health assistant!

Confused about your symptoms? AuaChat can help! Powered by cutting-edge Natural Language Processing (NLP), AuaChat analyzes your symptoms and provides insights and information.

Here's what AuaChat can do for you:

  • Understand your symptoms: Simply describe what you're experiencing, and AuaChat will leverage its advanced NLP capabilities to understand your concerns.
  • Offer potential explanations: AuaChat will provide a list of possible diagnoses based on your symptoms.

AuaChat is:

  • Fast and convenient: Get insights into your symptoms anytime, anywhere.
  • Easy to use: Simply chat with AuaChat in a natural way.
  • Informative: Gain valuable knowledge about your health.



Please note that AuaChat does not replace professional medical advice or diagnosis. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for any medical concerns.
